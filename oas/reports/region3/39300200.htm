Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Office of Inspector General Concerns Pertaining to Safeguards Over Medicaid Managed Care Programs," (A-03-93-00200)

August 27, 1993

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (2.92 MB). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

This final report points out that there is a need for improved safeguards over Medicaid managed care programs to reduce the risk of insolvency, some of the practices that oftentimes lead to insolvency, and poor quality of care.
