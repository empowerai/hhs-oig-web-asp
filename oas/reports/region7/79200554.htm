Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Review of General and Administrative, Fringe Benefits, and Other Costs Reported by North Kansas City Hospital," (A-07-92-00554)

November 16, 1992

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (1.29 MB). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

This report presents the results of our review of the general and administrative (G&A), fringe benefits, and other costs reported by North Kansas City Hospital (NKCH), North Kansas City, Missouri. Our review showed that reported costs totaling $273,585, subject to Medicare participation, were unallowable under the Medicare cost reimbursement principles since these costs were unrelated to patient care.
