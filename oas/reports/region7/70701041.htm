Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Mutual of Omaha's Medicare Final Administrative Cost Proposals for Fiscal Years 2004 Through 2006," (A-07-07-01041)

November 1, 2007

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (309 kb). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

Mutual reported expenditures that substantially complied with the Federal Acquisition Regulation and the Medicare contract provisions.  However, Mutual reported, in its cost proposals, $1,266 that was not allowable.  These costs were for entertainment ($840) and lobbying ($426), and as such, did not meet the criteria for Federal reimbursement.  We recommended that Mutual refund to the Federal Government the $1,266 of unallowable costs.  Mutual agreed with our findings.
