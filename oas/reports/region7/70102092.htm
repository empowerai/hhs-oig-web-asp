Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Regence Blue Cross Blue Shield of Utah for Part A and Part B of the Medicare Program," (A-07-01-02092)

May 29, 2001

Complete Text of Report  is available in PDF format (1.62 MB). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

This report covers the audit of administrative costs claimed by Regence Blue Cross Blue Shield of Utah for Part A and Part B of the Medicare Program for the Period October 1, 1997 through September 30, 1999.  For the period audited, Regence Blue Cross Blue Shield of Utah claimed administrative costs of $4,915,861 for Part A and $9,339.688 for Part B.  There were no findings or recommendations as a result of the audit.
