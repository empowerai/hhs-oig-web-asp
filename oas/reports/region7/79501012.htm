Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Retroactive Claim for Title IV-A Emergency Assistance Submitted by the Missouri Department of Social Services," (A-07-95-01012)

October 25, 1995

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (32 KB). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

This final report discusses the allowability of a prior quarter claim totaling $2,529,894 in Federal financial participation (FFP) Submitted by the Missouri Department of Social Services (DSS) for reimbursement under the Title IV-A Emergency Assistance Program (EA). The report covered retroactive costs for the period October 1, 1992 through June 30, 1994. We found that the retroactive claim for $2,529,894 in FFP was documented and allowable for reimbursement.
