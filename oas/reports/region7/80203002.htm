Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Review of Overpayment Collections by Aid to Families With Dependent Children (AFDC) by South Dakota Department of Social Services (DSS)," (A-08-02-03002)

December 5, 2001

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (261 KB). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

The objective of our review was to determine whether the DSS remitted the Federal share of AFDC overpayment collections to the Federal government after the program was repealed. We determined the DSS has adequate systems in place to identify, collect, account for, and remit the Federal share of AFDC overpayments.
