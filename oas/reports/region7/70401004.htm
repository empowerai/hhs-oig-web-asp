Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Managed Care Family Planning Claimed by Missouri," (A-07-04-01004)

January 12, 2005

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (128 kb). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

The objective of our audit was to determine if Missouri’s methodology to claim Medicaid family planning costs under managed care complied with Federal laws and regulations.  We found that Missouri’s methodology for identifying family planning generally complied with Federal laws and regulations in place during the audit period.  Therefore, we have no recommendations for corrective actions.
