Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Review of Disaster-Related Medicare Claims Submitted by The Mobility Depot for Calendar Year 2005," (A-06-07-00079)

June 26, 2008

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (281 kb). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

We found that the beneficiaries identified on 40 selected disaster-related Medicare claims submitted by The Mobility Depot were eligible for replacement durable medical equipment (DME) and provided with allowable DME.  The Mobility Depot, located in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, supplied this DME to beneficiaries affected by Hurricanes Katrina and Rita.  This report contains no recommendations.
