Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"States' Practices and Perspectives for Assessing Fees for Child Support Services to Applicants Not Receiving Aid to Families with Dependent Children," (A-06-91-00048)

July 8, 1992

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (876 kb). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

This final report provides the results of our review of States' and territories practices in establishing Child Support Enforcement (CSE) fees for non-Aid to Families with Dependent Children (non-AFDC) cases. Our review showed that revenues raised from application fees collected for non-AFDC cases did not meet the anticipated fees and revenues envisioned by the Congressional Budget Office.
