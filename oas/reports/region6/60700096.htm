Review of Louisiana Bioterrorism Hospital Preparedness Program - Louisiana Hospital Association

September 08, 2008 | Audit A-06-07-00096

Executive Summary

Hospitals improperly charged the Bioterrorism Hospital Preparedness Program $110,000 by claiming unallowable and undocumented costs and by spending funds more than 90 days after the Federal program funding period ended.
