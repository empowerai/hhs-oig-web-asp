Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Review of High-Dollar Payments for Medicare Part A Outpatient Claims Processed by TriSpan Health Services for the Period January 1, 2003, Through December 31, 2003,” (A-06-07-00100)

March 25, 2008

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (480 kb). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

None of the four high-dollar payments ($50,000 or more) that TriSpan Health Services (TriSpan) made for outpatient services in calendar year 2003 were appropriate.  Our analysis indicated that the resulting overpayment totaled $227,547, of which $104,521 was recovered prior to the end of our audit.  TriSpan is a Medicare Part A fiscal intermediary serving more than 1,800 Medicare providers in Mississippi, Louisiana, and Missouri.

We recommended that TriSpan inform us of the status of the recovery of the $123,026 in identified overpayments and use the results of this audit in its provider education activities.  TriSpan agreed with our recommendations.
