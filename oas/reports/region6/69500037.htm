EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

This report provides you with the results of the Office of Inspector General (OIG) Office of Audit Services (OAS) Review of Oklahoma Claims for Contract Training and Administrative Costs. The purpose of our review was to determine the allowability of training costs claimed by the Oklahoma Department of Human Services (State) under Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) programs. Our review covered the period July 1, 1993 through June 30, 1994.
