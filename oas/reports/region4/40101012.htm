Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Audit of Graduate Medical Education Costs at the University of North Carolina Hospitals, Chapel Hill, North Carolina," (A-04-01-01012)

June 5, 2002

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (591 Kb). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

This final audit report shows that the Hospital’s counts of intern and resident full-time equivalents were materially correct and the Hospital’s controls over the accumulation and computation of the full time employees appeared to be adequate. Therefore, this report contains no recommendations for the hospital to address.
