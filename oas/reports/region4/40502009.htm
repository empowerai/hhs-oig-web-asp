Review of Comprehensive Outpatient Rehabilitation Facility Therapy Services Provided by Ultimate Rehabilitation Agency, Inc.

September 18, 2008 | Audit (A-04-05-02009)

Executive Summary

For calendar year 2003, we estimated that Ultimate Rehabilitation Facility Inc. received $1.4 million for outpatient therapy services that did not meet Medicare reimbursement requirements.
