Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Review of Title II of the Ryan White Comprehensive AIDS Resources Emergency Act at the Florida Department of Health," (A-04-03-08014)

June 30, 2004

Complete Text of Report is available in 492 PDF format (392 kb). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

The objectives of this audit were to determine if the Health Department:  met key service delivery performance goals and complied with applicable program and cost requirements; used cost-savings strategies when purchasing AIDS Drug Assistance Program (ADAP) drugs.  The Health Department exceeded its service delivery performance goals in terms of the number of clients served; complied with program requirements regarding non-Federal matching funds, previous year’s expenditures, spending caps, and public involvement; and used cost-savings strategies to purchase ADAP drugs at discounted prices.
