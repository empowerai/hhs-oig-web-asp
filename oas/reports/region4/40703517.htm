Allowability of Costs Claimed for Reimbursement Under Mississippi's Bioterrorism and Emergency Preparedness Programs for the Period August 31, 2004, Through August 30, 2006

September 15, 2008 | Audit (A-04-07-03517)

Executive Summary

For the period August 31, 2004, through August 30, 2006, Mississippi claimed $23.8 million for reimbursement under the Bioterrorism and Emergency Preparedness Programs that was allowable, allocable, and reasonable. Accordingly, this report contains no recommendations.
