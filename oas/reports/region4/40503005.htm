Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Audit of Pathology Laboratory Services Claimed by Florida Urology Physicians, P.A. for the Period September Through December 2004," (A-04-05-03005)

June 28, 2007

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (559 kb). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

The audit objectives to (1) determine whether Florida Urology Physicians, P.A. (the Practice) claimed reimbursement for pathology laboratory services in accordance with Medicare Part B requirements from September through December 2004 and (2) analyze the Practice's utilization and pattern for pathology services.  The Practice’s claims for pathology laboratory services generally complied with Medicare Part B requirements.  We noted an increase in the number of pathology services requested and performed after the Practice opened its own laboratory, which increased costs to the Medicare program.  However, the Practice explained that the increased number of tissue examination requests were an attempt to more fully meet the needs of its patients.  The audit report did not contain any recommendations for the Practice.
