Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Review of Administrative Costs - Medicare Parts A and B - Aetna Life Insurance Company," (A-01-97-00529)

October 21, 1998

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (2.29 MB). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

This final audit report covers administrative costs claimed by the Aetna Life Insurance Company for administering Part A and Part B of the Medicare program for fiscal years 1995 through 1997. Administrative costs reported for the above period totaled approximately $323 million. The final report points out unallowable costs of and recommends financial adjustments for $2.9 million. The Aetna agreed to the financial adjustment for $2.9 million.
