Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Great Brook Valley Health Center Community Health Center Cluster Grant Year Ended May 31, 2004," (A-01-05-01500)

May 4, 2005

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (98 kb). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

The objectives of our review were to obtain an understanding of the financial and program performance for the Federal grants at Great Brook Valley Health Center and to ensure that Federal funds were used in accordance with the terms of the grants.  We found the reported costs and program performance for FY 2004 to be in compliance with applicable Federal regulations.  Accordingly, we have no further comments or recommendations to make regarding this review.
