Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Review of Quality Improvement Organization in Rhode Island," (A-01-06-00507)

August 23, 2007

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (232 kb). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

The Senate Finance Committee requested that OIG assess the fiscal integrity of the Medicare Quality Improvement Organizations (QIO) with respect to six specified fiscal integrity areas.

Of the $3.7 million of costs reviewed related to the six areas, the Rhode Island QIO, Rhode Island Quality Partners, provided adequate documentation to support the costs claimed.  Accordingly, this report contains no recommendations.
