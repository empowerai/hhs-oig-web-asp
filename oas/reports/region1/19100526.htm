Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Cost of Dialysis-Related Drugs," (A-01-91-00526)

October 20, 1992

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (1.37 MB). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

This final report summarizes the results of our review of the Health Care Financing Administration's (HCFA) proposal to change the methodology for reimbursing separately billable drugs under Medicare's end stage renal disease (ESRD) program. Our results indicated that dialysis facilities purchase separately billable drugs significantly below the average wholesale price.
