Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Audit of Medicaid Payments for Skilled Professional Medical Personnel Reimbursed at Enhanced Rates October 1, 2002 Through September 30, 2003 Office of Vermont Health Access," (A-01-04-00008)

March 29, 2005

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (208 kb). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

We found that the State of Vermont improperly claimed Medicaid costs related to skilled professional medical personnel totaling $262,219 for Federal fiscal year 2003.  The State did not have adequate procedures in place to ensure that only eligible individuals and allowable expenses were claimed for enhanced Federal funding in accordance with Federal regulations or that all costs claimed were appropriate charges to the Medicaid program. We recommended that the State refund $262,219 for the Federal share of unallowable Medicaid costs and implement procedures to ensure that future claims are eligible for the enhanced Federal funding rate.  State officials agreed with our recommendations.
