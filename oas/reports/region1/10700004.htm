Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Review of Medicaid Credit Balances at Baystate Medical Center for the Period Ending June 30, 2006," (A-01-07-00004)

July 25, 2007

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (755 kb). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

Our objective was to determine whether the credit balances recorded in Baystate Medical Center’s (the Hospital) accounting records for inpatient and outpatient services for Medicaid beneficiaries represented overpayments more than 60 days old that the Hospital should have returned to the Medicaid program.  We identified 267 overpayments more than 60 days old that the Hospital should have returned to the Medicaid program.  As a result, the Hospital did not promptly return Medicaid overpayments totaling $184,835 ($92,418 Federal share) to the Medicaid program in accordance with State Medicaid requirements.  We recommended that the Hospital return these overpayments to the Massachusetts Medicaid program and continue efforts to identify and return all overpayments to Medicaid in accordance with State requirements.  The Hospital agreed substantially with our observations and findings.
