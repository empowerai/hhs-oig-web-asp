Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Review of the State of New Jersey’s Fiscal Year 1994 Pension Fund Withdrawal," (A-02-01-02005)

August 17, 2001

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (563 KB). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

In 1994 New Jersey, based on changes to its State law, withdrew $180.2 million from State employees’ pension programs and deposited the funds into the State’s General Fund.  This final report points out that the State did not credit the Federal Government with its share of the pension expenses originally funded by Federal programs, grants, and contracts. The amount owed the Federal Government, including interest, totaled about $9.7 million. We recommended the State return this amount to the Federal Government. The State concurred, and issued a check to the Department of Health and Human Services for the $9.7 million in July 2001.
