Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Review Of Cost Sharing and Cost Transfers At The University of Rochester For The Period July 1, 2002 to June 30, 2003," (A-02-05-02000)

September 21, 2005

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (531 kb). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

This final report states that the University of Rochester has procedures in place to document cost sharing; removed salary above the cap when submitting grant applications and claims for Federal reimbursement for NIH projects and properly included salary above the cap in the calculation of the F&A cost rate; developed and implemented written cost transfer procedures and controls; and, sufficiently justified and supported cost transfers to NIH projects in accordance with OMB Circular No. A-21 and A-110, as well as the NIH Grant's Policy.  The report contained no recommendations.
