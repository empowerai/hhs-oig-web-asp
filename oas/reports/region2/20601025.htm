Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Review of Nursing Education Payments to St. Joseph's Hospital Health Center for the Period January 1, 2004, Through December 31, 2004," (A-02-06-01025)

June 17, 2008

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (169 kb). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

For calendar year 2004, St. Joseph’s Hospital Health Center’s nursing education program met both Medicare eligibility and reimbursement requirements.  As a result, the report contains no recommendations.
