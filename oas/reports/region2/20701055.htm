Follow-Up Audit of the Medicaid Drug Rebate Program in New York

September 08, 2008 | Audit A-02-07-01055

Executive Summary

The State agency implemented the recommendations from our prior audit of New York's Medicaid drug rebate program and established controls that greatly improved the overall operation of the program. The State agency is developing improvements to its system to address outstanding or disputed rebates for the period January 1, 1991, through March 31, 1999.
