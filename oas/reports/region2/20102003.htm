Department of Health and Human Services

"Review of the Administration for the Children and Families Discretionary Grant Number 90CT0010 Awarded to Fordham University for the Period September 30, 1997  Through September 29, 2000," (A-02-01-02003)

May 22, 2002

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (2.33 MB). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

This final report provides the results of our review of the Administration for Children and Families Discretionary Grant Number 90CT0010 awarded to Fordham University for the period September 30, 1997 through September 29, 2000.  The objective was to evaluate the grantee's performance in achieving project objectives and complying with terms and conditions of the grant project.  In general, Fordham met the project goals of improving child welfare services through interdisciplinary training to public agency workers and supervisors and performing other project tasks.  However, we found several exceptions with respect to compliance, with the terms and conditions of the grant.  Specifically, Fordham awarded a scholarship to a recipient who did not meet the project qualifications; Fordham did not publish articles pertaining to the project lessons learned and conference proceedings in keeping with the approved project goals, and course evaluations and attendance forms were not always retained by Fordham.  We recommended a financial adjustment, compliance with all conditions and terms of future grants and establishment of record retention policies.
