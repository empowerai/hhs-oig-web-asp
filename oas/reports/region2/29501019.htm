Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Audit of the Home Office Cost Statement of Staff Builders, Inc. for the Fiscal Year Ended February 28, 1994," (A-02-95-01019)

August 19, 1998

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (870 KB). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1142.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

This final audit report points out that Staff Builders, Inc. improperly claimed Medicare reimbursements for home office costs totaling approximately $3.4 million for Fiscal Year 1994. The improper claims were related to costs that were; improperly allocated, primarily related to private lines of business, not related to patient care, not reasonable, inadequately documented, and not properly offset by related rebates and interest income. We recommended a financial adjustment and procedural improvements to strengthen controls applicable to the preparation of future home office cost statements.
