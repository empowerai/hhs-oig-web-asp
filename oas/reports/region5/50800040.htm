Review of Wisconsin's Non-Emergency Medical Transportation Costs for Services Provided by American United Taxicab, Inc. for January 1 Through December 31, 2005

August 04, 2008 | Audit A-05-08-00040

Executive Summary

For the period January 1 through December 31, 2005, we estimated that Wisconsin claimed $694,000 ($347,000 Federal share) in Medicaid reimbursement for non-emergency medical transportation services provided by American United Taxicab on dates when beneficiaries did not receive Medicaid-covered services.
