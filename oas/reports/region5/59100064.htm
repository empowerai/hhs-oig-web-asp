Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Report on the Audit of Administrative Costs Incurred by Nationwide Mutual Insurance Company Under Part B of the Health Insurance for the Aged and Disabled Program," (A-05-91-00064)

October 24, 1991

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (1.16 mb). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

This final audit report prepared under audit contract with a certified public accounting firm presents the results of the administrative costs claimed by Nationwide Mutual Insurance Company (Nationwide). The review disclosed that administrative costs claimed by Nationwide for Health Insurance for the Aged and Disabled program contained amounts recommended for financial adjustments of $3.3 million. We are recommending that Nationwide make appropriate financial adjustments in these amounts.
