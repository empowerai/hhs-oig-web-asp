Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Administrative Costs Incurred Under Part A and Part B of the Health Insurance for the Aged and Disabled Program - Health Care Service Corporation, Chicago, Illinois," (A-05-92-00108)

December 9, 1992

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (5.78 mb). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

The firm of Doshi and Associates, P.C., under contract with the Office of Inspector General, conducted an audit of Medicare administrative costs incurred by Health Care Service Corporation (HCSC). The audit covered the costs claimed on HCSC's final administrative cost proposals (FACP) for the Fiscal Years (FY) 1987 through 1990. The audit resulted in cost questioned of $1,121,889.
