Department of Health and Human Services

Office of Inspector General -- AUDIT

"Review of Medicare Credit Balances in Wisconsin," (A-05-91-00128)

May 7, 1992

Complete Text of Report is available in PDF format (2.3 mb). Copies can also be obtained by contacting the Office of Public Affairs at 202-619-1343.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:

This final audit report discloses that Medicare accounts receivable credit balances included unidentified overpayments totaling an estimated $919,826 in the State of Wisconsin. The overpayments occurred because both hospitals and Blue Cross and Blue Shield United of Wisconsin (BCBSUW) did not adequately review credit balances and process adjustments timely. We are recommending recovery of the overpayments and procedural improvements to ensure that the hospitals and BCBSUW perform more adequate and timely reviews.
