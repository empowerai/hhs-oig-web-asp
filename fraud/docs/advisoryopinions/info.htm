INFORMATION SHEET REGARDING THE OIG ADVISORY OPINION PROCESS

January 5, 2001

The Office of Inspector General is again accepting advisory opinion requests, in accordance with section 543 of H.R. 5661 as enacted by the Omnibus Consolidated and Emergency Supplemental Appropriations for Fiscal Year 2001 (Public Law 106-554).

Requests for advisory opinions should comply with the regulations set forth in 42 CFR part 1008. These regulations, as well as a preliminary checklist listing the requirements for a complete request, can be found at http://www.oig.hhs.gov
